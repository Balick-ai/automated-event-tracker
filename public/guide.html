Searches

Finding Events

Discover
Ticketmaster + Eventbrite — Fast, free, unlimited. Genre-based + venue-specific search in 10 US cities.
AI Search
Google Gemini AI — Searches RA, DICE, edmtrain, Songkick, and venue sites. 1 free search/week; add your own Gemini key for unlimited.

An optional date range filter lets you narrow searches to specific dates. Default: next 6 months.

Results appear in a Discovery Queue where you can Add, Edit & Add, or Dismiss each event. "Add All New" bulk-adds everything at once.

Supported cities for venue search: New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Chicago, Las Vegas, San Francisco, Denver, Detroit, Austin, Seattle. Other cities get genre-based search + AI Search.

Important

Your Data

Your shows are stored in your browser. There are no accounts and no server database. Clearing your browser data or cache will delete your shows.

Google Calendar is your backup. Once shows are synced, they persist in your Google Calendar even if browser data is cleared. Sync regularly to keep your events safe.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is this free?

Yes. All features are free. AI Search has a weekly limit but you can add your own free Gemini key for unlimited use.

Does my data persist?

Yes, across sessions. But clearing browser data deletes everything. Sync to Google Calendar as a backup.

Can others see my shows?

No. Each person has their own private data in their browser.

What if a show isn't found?

Add it manually with the + button. Not all events are on Ticketmaster — AI Search catches more, and you can always add from flyers or social media.

My city isn't supported for venue search?

Genre-based search works everywhere. AI Search also works for any city. Only venue-specific search is limited to supported cities.
